Our Services

Industrial Cafeteria Meals

At Palki Food Services, we extend our expertise beyond corporate catering to cater to the unique needs of industrial settings. Our industrial catering services encompass both in-house production and supply from our base kitchen to factories, ensuring timely delivery of nourishing meals.

Designed specifically for the workforce at manufacturing plants spanning industries such as automobile, telecom, mobile parts, engineering products, and more, our offerings prioritize quality, variety, and nutritional value. With a focus on fueling productivity and promoting employee well-being, Palki Food Services is your trusted partner in industrial catering excellence.

Corporate Cafeteria Meals

At Palki Food Services, we specialize in corporate catering, offering a comprehensive range of food services tailored to the needs of corporate offices, MNC headquarters, and factories. Our in-house production ensures the highest quality meals, prepared in our state-of-the-art kitchen facility spanning 2500 sq. ft.
